Fun in The Blanks

How to Play

Fun in the Blanks is a creatvie game that allows you and a child create their own fairy tale story.

To play open up any chapter. Ask the child for requested in the prompts. Enter their answer. Once the form is filled out, click the "Build Your Story" button. The game will then take your words and put them into the story. or play alone.

Read the story with the child.
